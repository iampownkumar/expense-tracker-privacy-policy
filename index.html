Section 01

Who We Are

Kora Expense Tracker is an independent Android application developed by Pownkumar A. (operating as Korelium), based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. The app is a personal finance management tool with a strict local-first architecture — no cloud infrastructure, no remote database, no user accounts.

This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding the Android application available on the Google Play Store. By installing and using Kora Expense Tracker, you agree to the terms described here.

Section 02

Data We Collect & Store

All data in Kora Expense Tracker is created by you, stored on your device, and never transmitted anywhere. The following table describes each data type:

Data Type Where It's Stored Sent to Us?
Transaction records (amount, date, category, notes) Device internal storage (SharedPreferences) Never
Account & balance information Device internal storage (SharedPreferences) Never
Credit card details (limit, due date, outstanding) Device internal storage (SharedPreferences) Never
Receipt / bill photos you capture or attach Device local file system (app-accessible path) Never
App preferences and settings Device internal storage (SharedPreferences) Never
CSV exports you generate Your device's Downloads folder Device only
Device identifiers, IP address, location Not collected. We do not access any of these.
Important: Because all data is stored locally on your device, uninstalling the app will permanently erase all your data unless you have created a CSV backup export. We strongly recommend using the Export feature regularly.
Section 03

Android Permissions Explained

Kora Expense Tracker requests the following Android system permissions. Each permission is requested only when you take an explicit action that requires it — the app never requests permissions in the background.

📷
Camera
android.permission.CAMERA

Used exclusively to photograph physical receipts or bills and attach them to a transaction entry for your own record-keeping. The camera is never accessed passively or in the background. No images are uploaded anywhere — they are saved to your device's local file system only.

Only triggered when you tap "Take Photo" inside the transaction editor.
🗂️
Read External Storage
android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

Used to allow you to pick an existing image from your photo gallery to attach to a transaction as a receipt. We only read the specific file you select through the Android system file picker — we do not scan your gallery or access any other files.

Only triggered when you tap "Choose from Gallery" inside the transaction editor.
💾
Write External Storage
android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

Required to save CSV backup files and PDF reports to your device's Downloads folder, so you can easily find and share your own exported data. This permission is not used for any other purpose — we do not write any other files to your storage.

Only triggered when you initiate an Export or Generate Report action.
Your control: You can revoke any of these permissions at any time via Android Settings → Apps → Kora Expense Tracker → Permissions. Revoking a permission only disables that specific feature — the rest of the app continues to function normally.
Section 04

Third-Party Services & SDKs

Kora Expense Tracker does not integrate any third-party services, libraries, or SDKs that collect, process, or transmit user data. Specifically, the app contains:

  • ✗ No Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or Crashlytics
  • ✗ No AdMob, AdSense, or any advertising network
  • ✗ No Facebook SDK, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or similar analytics tools
  • ✗ No push notification services that transmit device tokens to a server
  • ✗ No crash reporting tools that send data off-device

The only network activity the app may perform is loading fonts from Google Fonts during the privacy policy web page display (this document) — the app itself makes zero network requests.

Section 05

Children's Privacy

Kora Expense Tracker is a personal finance management tool intended for users aged 13 and above. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no data whatsoever, there is no risk of inadvertent collection from any age group.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used this app, please note that all data remains entirely on the device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data through Android Settings.

Section 06

Data Security

Because all your data is stored locally on your device, the security of your financial information is inherently tied to the security of your device itself. We strongly recommend:

  • 🔒Enabling a screen lock (PIN, pattern, fingerprint, or face unlock) on your Android device.
  • 💾Regularly using the in-app Export feature to back up your data to a CSV file, which you can store in a secure location.
  • 🔄Keeping the app updated to the latest version available on the Play Store.
  • 🚫Not installing the app on shared or rooted devices where other apps may have elevated access to local storage.

We do not implement server-side security measures because we do not operate any servers. Your data never leaves your device through our application.

Section 07

Your Rights & Controls

As all data is stored locally, you retain full and complete control at all times:

  • 🗑️
    Delete any record: Use the swipe-to-delete gesture on any transaction to permanently remove it from your device.
  • 📤
    Export your data: Use the Export feature to download a CSV copy of all your transactions to your Downloads folder.
  • 🧹
    Erase everything: Go to Android Settings → Apps → Kora Expense Tracker → Storage → Clear Data. This permanently deletes all app data from your device.
  • 🗂️
    Uninstall: Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently. Export first if you want to keep a record.
  • 📷
    Revoke permissions: Revoke Camera or Storage access any time via Android Settings → Apps → Kora Expense Tracker → Permissions.
